About

Who we are and What we do:

Who we are

What we do

Association for Teaching Excellence (ATE) stands at the forefront of empowering educators and shaping the future of education through innovative pedagogy, collaboration, and community building. We aim to harness the transformative power of technology, research, and innovation across India to build an inclusive community of professionals joining from every educational sector—spanning primary education to higher education and extending into industry partnerships.

We are proud to be a member-led charity, deeply rooted in and for the sector, consistently collaborating with partners globally. Our mission, deeply ingrained within our charitable objectives, is to elevate educational practices by amplifying, exploring, and circulating knowledge in the realm of teaching excellence for the collective betterment of society. We are persistent in our commitment to nurturing professional growth of educators and providing recognition in the field. Our membership is open to anyone who harbours a passion for harnessing an innovative yet research informed approach to enhance learning, teaching, and assessment. We aim to elevate educational provisions, ensuring that both staff and students benefit, ultimately contributing to the betterment of society as a whole.

Member-led Organisation

Our core activities revolve around delivering exceptional membership services that are designed to uphold our strategic goals while also securing the financial sustainability for our organisation as an independent entity.

ATE is driven by member-led initiatives to augment the stature of higher education on both domestic and international platforms. Our work is shaped by key objectives that reflect our members' needs and the broader aspirations of the educational community:

  • Empowering Educators 

    We support the endeavours of our members by providing resources, guidance, and opportunities to lead and innovate within the education sector.

  • Building Trust in Education

    We are committed to reinforcing the reliability and quality of the educational system, ensuring it serves the needs of society and upholds its esteem.

  • Promoting Inclusivity

    Recognising the varied needs of our educators and sector, we focus on bridging educational divides and fostering an environment where everyone can learn and grow equally.
